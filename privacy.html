Overview

Bill Tracker Hub helps users view liability information from linked financial accounts. This policy describes what information is processed and how it is used.

Information Processed

  • User identifier used by the app backend for account linking.
  • Plaid link and item metadata required to maintain account connections.
  • Liability data returned by Plaid, such as balances, amount due, and due dates.

How Information Is Used

  • To create and exchange Plaid Link tokens.
  • To retrieve and display liability data in the iOS app.
  • To maintain linked account state for returning users.

Data Sharing

Bill Tracker Hub uses Plaid to facilitate financial data connectivity. Data sharing with institutions and Plaid is governed by user consent and Plaid's platform.

Data Retention

Linked account and liability snapshot data may be stored by the backend service to support app functionality. Retention periods and deletion workflows are subject to ongoing product and compliance updates.

Security

  • Transport security uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Authentication and institution connection flows are handled through Plaid Link.

TYDES

Bill and Cash Flow Tracking With Plaid Connectivity

TYDES is an iOS application for connecting financial accounts, tracking liabilities and due dates, and planning near-term cash flow.

Privacy Policy Privacy Choices

What The App Does

  • Connects authorized financial accounts through Plaid.
  • Requests clear consent before connecting financial accounts.
  • Supports secure sign-in and supported institution connection flows.
  • Shows Dashboard, Accounts, Schedule, and Cash Flow views for balances, due dates, statement status, and near-term planning.

Security and Data Handling

  • TYDES uses encrypted connections to protect data in transit.
  • Sign in with Apple is used for account authentication.
  • Sensitive connected-account data is protected with encryption at rest.
  • TYDES uses authorized financial data only to support bill tracking, liability insights, and Cash Flow planning.

Support

For support requests, feature requests, or data questions, use the TYDES support contact listed in the app or App Store listing.

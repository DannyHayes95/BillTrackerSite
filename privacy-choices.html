Overview

This page explains how to request privacy support, account-data deletion help, or linked account disconnection support.

Available Requests

  • Request help disconnecting linked financial institutions.
  • Request deletion of account-associated app data from active TYDES systems.
  • Request clarification about Plaid data consent records associated with your account.
  • Request clarification about what data categories are processed.
  • Request correction support if app-controlled information appears inaccurate.

How To Submit A Request

  1. Open a support issue at TYDES support issues.
  2. Use a subject line that starts with Privacy Request.
  3. Describe the request type (disconnect, deletion, correction, or data question).
  4. Request a private contact path if identity verification is required.

Do not include sensitive personal or financial account numbers in public issue text.

Plaid Portal Option

Users may also manage certain Plaid-side connection and data controls directly in Plaid Portal.

When app-side disconnection or verified deletion is processed, TYDES attempts to remove the linked Plaid Item before deleting local encrypted Plaid access tokens and active app records.

Request Handling Targets

  • Target acknowledgment window: within 10 business days.
  • Target completion window: within 30 business days after identity verification and scope confirmation.

Completed deletion requests remove active app records such as authentication sessions, planning state, Plaid link metadata, encrypted Plaid access tokens, consent records, liability data, and related coverage records. Complex requests may require additional time when needed for security, fraud prevention, platform outage handling, or legal compliance.
